Basil Thyme

About Us

Hello, we are Basil & Thyme, a top-notch vegetarian gastronomic experience in Amsterdam. Organic food, freshly squeezed juices, homegrown falafel, we have it all.

Organic Food

All our food, from vegetables to pies, are fresh, organic and local. Our food is provided by farmers in and around Amsterdam, as well as some of our own produce. Good for your health, the environment, and it tastes better!

Friend Zone

We don’t have WiFi, so you can just put your phone down and enjoy each other’s company! Not enough to talk about? We provide games you can play before and after dinner.
